# Photography & Media Consent — Demo Content

Copy the HTML below into the **Photography / Media Consent** editor in Event Settings.

---

```html
<h2>Photography &amp; Media Consent</h2>

<h3>1. Image &amp; Video Recording</h3>
<p>The Organiser and its authorised photographers may take <strong>photographs and video recordings</strong> during the event for documentation and promotional purposes.</p>

<h3>2. Use of Materials</h3>
<p>By attending and checking this box, you consent to the use of your likeness in:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Event recaps and highlight videos</li>
  <li>Social media posts (LinkedIn, X, Instagram, YouTube)</li>
  <li>The Organiser's website and newsletters</li>
  <li>Future promotional materials for similar events</li>
</ul>

<h3>3. Opt-Out</h3>
<p>If you prefer <strong>not</strong> to be photographed, please collect a "no photo" badge at the registration desk. The Organiser will make reasonable efforts to exclude you from published materials.</p>

<h3>4. Withdrawal</h3>
<p>You may withdraw this consent at any time by contacting <strong>media@swiss-robotics.org</strong>. Upon withdrawal, we will remove identifiable images within 30 days where technically feasible.</p>

<h3>5. Third-Party Sharing</h3>
<p>Images may be shared with event sponsors or media partners exclusively for event-related coverage. They will not be sold or used for unrelated commercial purposes.</p>
```
