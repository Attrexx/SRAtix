# Photography &amp; Media Consent — Demo / Starter Content

Copy the HTML below into the **Photography / Media Consent** editor in Event Settings.

---

```html
<h2>Photography &amp; Media Consent</h2>

<p><em>Version 1.0</em></p>

<p>The Swiss Robotics Association ("SRA", "Organiser") may arrange for <strong>photography, video recording, and live streaming</strong> during the Event for documentation, communication, and promotional purposes.</p>

<h3>1. What We Record</h3>
<p>Professional photographers and videographers authorised by the Organiser may capture:</p>
<ul>
  <li>General event atmosphere and venue images</li>
  <li>Keynote speeches, panel discussions, and presentations</li>
  <li>Exhibition areas, booths, and demonstrations</li>
  <li>Networking sessions and social activities</li>
  <li>Individual and group photos of attendees</li>
</ul>

<h3>2. How We Use the Materials</h3>
<p>By checking the photography consent box during registration, you grant the SRA a <strong>non-exclusive, royalty-free, worldwide licence</strong> to use photographs, video recordings, and other media in which you appear for the following purposes:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Event documentation</strong> — recap articles, event reports, and annual review publications</li>
  <li><strong>Digital communications</strong> — the SRA website (<em>swiss-robotics.org</em>), newsletters, and email communications</li>
  <li><strong>Social media</strong> — SRA accounts on LinkedIn, X (Twitter), Instagram, YouTube, and other platforms</li>
  <li><strong>Promotional materials</strong> — brochures, presentations, and advertisements for future SRA events</li>
  <li><strong>Press and media</strong> — press releases shared with media partners covering the event</li>
</ul>
<p>Materials may include your <strong>name, job title, and organisation</strong> in captions where editorially relevant.</p>

<h3>3. Speaker &amp; Presenter Content</h3>
<p>If you are a speaker or presenter, your talk may be recorded and published (in whole or in part) on the SRA website and YouTube channel for educational and promotional purposes. Speakers will be informed separately of any recording arrangements and may opt out of publication.</p>

<h3>4. Opting Out</h3>
<p>If you prefer <strong>not to be photographed or recorded</strong>:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Collect a <strong>"No Photo" badge sticker</strong> at the registration desk upon check-in</li>
  <li>Inform event staff or photographers directly — they will respect your request</li>
</ul>
<p>The Organiser will make <strong>reasonable efforts</strong> to exclude identifiable images of Participants wearing the "No Photo" indicator from all published materials. However, in large event settings, incidental inclusion in wide-angle or crowd shots may occur.</p>

<h3>5. Third-Party Sharing</h3>
<p>Event media may be shared with:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Event sponsors</strong> — for co-branded event coverage and sponsor reports</li>
  <li><strong>Media partners</strong> — for press coverage of the event</li>
  <li><strong>Speakers</strong> — recordings of their own presentations</li>
</ul>
<p>Media will <strong>not</strong> be sold to third parties or used for purposes unrelated to SRA activities.</p>

<h3>6. Attendee-Generated Content</h3>
<p>Participants are welcome to take their own photos and videos at the Event for personal and professional use, provided they:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Respect the wishes of other attendees who decline to be photographed</li>
  <li>Do not record in areas where recording is restricted (as announced by the Organiser)</li>
  <li>Comply with the Code of Conduct regarding consent and respectful behaviour</li>
</ul>
<p>The SRA encourages attendees to share their event experience on social media using the official event hashtag.</p>

<h3>7. Withdrawal of Consent</h3>
<p>You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting <strong>media@swiss-robotics.org</strong>. Upon withdrawal:</p>
<ul>
  <li>We will <strong>remove clearly identifiable images</strong> of you from our website and social media channels within <strong>30 days</strong>, where technically feasible.</li>
  <li>Removal from printed materials already distributed, third-party reposts, or archived press coverage may not be possible.</li>
  <li>Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of use prior to the withdrawal.</li>
</ul>

<h3>8. Legal Basis</h3>
<p>Photography and media consent is collected as an <strong>explicit opt-in</strong> during registration, in accordance with Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR and Art. 31(1) nDSG. Your consent is recorded with a timestamp in our registration system.</p>
<p>For general event documentation (e.g., wide-angle venue shots not focused on identifiable individuals), we rely on our <strong>legitimate interest</strong> (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR / Art. 34 nDSG) in documenting and promoting our educational mission.</p>

<h3>9. Contact</h3>
<p>For questions about photography and media use:<br>
Swiss Robotics Association<br>
Zurich, Switzerland<br>
Email: <strong>media@swiss-robotics.org</strong></p>
```
