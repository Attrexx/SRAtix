# Code of Conduct — Demo Content

Copy the HTML below into the **Code of Conduct** editor in Event Settings.

---

```html
<h2>Code of Conduct</h2>

<p>All participants, speakers, sponsors, and volunteers at this event are required to agree to the following Code of Conduct. The Organiser will enforce this code throughout the event.</p>

<h3>Our Pledge</h3>
<p>We are committed to providing a <strong>harassment-free, inclusive experience</strong> for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, ethnicity, nationality, age, religion, or technical experience.</p>

<h3>Expected Behaviour</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Be respectful and considerate in all interactions</li>
  <li>Collaborate openly and constructively</li>
  <li>Refrain from demeaning, discriminatory, or harassing behaviour</li>
  <li>Alert event staff if you witness or experience unacceptable behaviour</li>
</ul>

<h3>Unacceptable Behaviour</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Intimidation, stalking, or following</li>
  <li>Offensive verbal comments related to personal characteristics</li>
  <li>Deliberate disruption of talks or other events</li>
  <li>Unwelcome physical contact or sexual attention</li>
  <li>Photography or recording without consent</li>
</ul>

<h3>Consequences</h3>
<p>Participants asked to stop any unacceptable behaviour are expected to comply immediately. Violations may result in removal from the event <strong>without a refund</strong> at the sole discretion of the Organiser.</p>

<h3>Reporting</h3>
<p>If you experience or witness a violation, please contact event staff or email <strong>conduct@swiss-robotics.org</strong>. All reports will be handled confidentially.</p>
```
