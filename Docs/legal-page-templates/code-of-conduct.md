# Code of Conduct — Demo / Starter Content

Copy the HTML below into the **Code of Conduct** editor in Event Settings.

---

```html
<h2>Event Code of Conduct</h2>

<p><em>Version 1.0</em></p>

<p>The Swiss Robotics Association ("SRA") is dedicated to providing a <strong>welcoming, safe, and inclusive event experience</strong> for all participants, regardless of background or identity.</p>
<p>All attendees, speakers, sponsors, exhibitors, volunteers, and staff ("Participants") are required to comply with this Code of Conduct at all event venues, associated social events, and online event channels.</p>

<h3>1. Our Values</h3>
<p>We believe that the robotics community thrives when its members treat each other with respect, curiosity, and professionalism. This Event is a space for:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Open exchange of ideas and knowledge sharing</li>
  <li>Constructive professional dialogue across disciplines</li>
  <li>Building connections between academia and industry</li>
  <li>Supporting diversity in the robotics community</li>
</ul>

<h3>2. Expected Behaviour</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Be <strong>respectful and considerate</strong> in all interactions, both in-person and online</li>
  <li>Use welcoming and inclusive language</li>
  <li>Be open to differing viewpoints and experiences</li>
  <li>Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully</li>
  <li>Respect the personal space and boundaries of others</li>
  <li>Follow the instructions of event staff and venue personnel</li>
  <li>Ask for consent before photographing or recording other attendees</li>
</ul>

<h3>3. Unacceptable Behaviour</h3>
<p>The following behaviours are considered violations of this Code of Conduct and will not be tolerated:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Harassment</strong> — including offensive verbal or written comments related to gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, ethnicity, nationality, race, age, religion, or level of technical experience</li>
  <li><strong>Intimidation or threats</strong> — including deliberate stalking, following, or sustained disruption of talks, presentations, or networking sessions</li>
  <li><strong>Unwelcome contact</strong> — physical contact or simulated physical contact without consent, and unwelcome sexual attention or advances</li>
  <li><strong>Disruptive behaviour</strong> — deliberate disruption of event proceedings, presentations, or activities</li>
  <li><strong>Discriminatory material</strong> — display of sexually explicit or violent material, or material promoting discrimination</li>
  <li><strong>Unauthorised recording</strong> — photography or recording of individuals without their consent, particularly in areas where consent has been explicitly restricted</li>
  <li><strong>Commercial misconduct</strong> — aggressive or deceptive sales practices, misrepresentation of products or services at exhibitor booths</li>
</ul>

<h3>4. Scope</h3>
<p>This Code of Conduct applies to all event-related spaces including:</p>
<ul>
  <li>The main conference and exhibition areas</li>
  <li>Workshop and breakout rooms</li>
  <li>Networking sessions, social events, and dinners associated with the Event</li>
  <li>Online channels and platforms associated with the Event</li>
  <li>Transport and accommodation arranged by the Organiser</li>
</ul>

<h3>5. Consequences</h3>
<p>Participants asked to stop any harassing or disruptive behaviour are expected to comply <strong>immediately</strong>.</p>
<p>The Organiser may, at its sole discretion, take any action deemed appropriate in response to a violation, including:</p>
<ul>
  <li>A verbal or written warning</li>
  <li>Removal from a specific session or area</li>
  <li>Expulsion from the Event <strong>without refund</strong></li>
  <li>Prohibition from future SRA events</li>
  <li>Reporting to relevant authorities where legally required</li>
</ul>

<h3>6. Reporting a Violation</h3>
<p>If you experience or witness behaviour that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it promptly. You may:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Speak to any member of <strong>event staff</strong> (identifiable by staff badges)</li>
  <li>Visit the <strong>registration or information desk</strong></li>
  <li>Email: <strong>conduct@swiss-robotics.org</strong></li>
</ul>
<p>All reports will be treated <strong>confidentially</strong>. We will not disclose the identity of the reporter without their consent, except where required by law.</p>
<p>The Organiser will investigate reports promptly and take appropriate action. Participants involved in a complaint will have the opportunity to be heard.</p>

<h3>7. No Retaliation</h3>
<p>Retaliation against anyone who reports a concern or participates in an investigation is strictly prohibited and will itself be treated as a Code of Conduct violation.</p>

<h3>8. Acknowledgement</h3>
<p>By registering for this Event, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.</p>

<h3>9. Contact</h3>
<p>Swiss Robotics Association<br>
Zurich, Switzerland<br>
Email: <strong>conduct@swiss-robotics.org</strong></p>
```
