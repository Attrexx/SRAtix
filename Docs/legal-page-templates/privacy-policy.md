# Privacy Policy — Demo Content

Copy the HTML below into the **Privacy Policy** editor in Event Settings.

---

```html
<h2>Privacy Policy</h2>

<h3>1. Data Controller</h3>
<p>The data controller is the Swiss Robotics Association, Zurich, Switzerland ("we", "us"). For questions regarding data processing, contact us at <strong>privacy@swiss-robotics.org</strong>.</p>

<h3>2. Data Collected</h3>
<p>When you register for our event we collect:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Name, email address, phone number</li>
  <li>Organisation and role</li>
  <li>Dietary requirements or accessibility needs (if provided)</li>
  <li>Payment information (processed by our payment provider)</li>
</ul>

<h3>3. Purpose &amp; Legal Basis</h3>
<p>We process your data to:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Fulfil the registration contract</strong> (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR / Art. 31 nDSG)</li>
  <li><strong>Comply with legal obligations</strong> (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR)</li>
  <li><strong>Send event-related communications</strong> based on our legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR)</li>
</ul>

<h3>4. Data Sharing</h3>
<p>We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared with:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Payment processors (Stripe) for transaction handling</li>
  <li>Email service providers for event communications</li>
  <li>Venue operators where required for access management</li>
</ul>

<h3>5. Retention</h3>
<p>Registration data is retained for <strong>2 years</strong> after the event for internal reporting, then deleted unless a longer retention period is required by law.</p>

<h3>6. Your Rights</h3>
<p>You have the right to access, rectify, delete, or port your data, and to object to processing. Contact <strong>privacy@swiss-robotics.org</strong> to exercise your rights.</p>

<h3>7. Applicable Law</h3>
<p>This policy is governed by the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (nDSG) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).</p>
```
