# Privacy Policy — Demo / Starter Content

Copy the HTML below into the **Privacy Policy** editor in Event Settings.  
Replace `[Event Date]` with the actual event date.

---

```html
<h2>Privacy Policy — Event Registration</h2>

<p><em>Version 1.0 — effective as of [Event Date]</em></p>

<p>The Swiss Robotics Association ("SRA", "we", "us") is committed to protecting the personal data of all event participants. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you register for and attend our events via the SRAtix platform.</p>

<h3>1. Data Controller</h3>
<p><strong>Swiss Robotics Association</strong><br>
Zurich, Switzerland<br>
Email: <strong>privacy@swiss-robotics.org</strong></p>
<p>For questions or requests regarding your personal data, please contact us at the email address above.</p>

<h3>2. Legal Framework</h3>
<p>This policy complies with the <strong>Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (nDSG)</strong>, effective 1 September 2023, and, where applicable to participants located in the European Economic Area, the <strong>EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)</strong>.</p>

<h3>3. Data Collected</h3>
<p>We collect and process the following categories of personal data during event registration:</p>

<p><strong>a) Required registration data:</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>First name, last name</li>
  <li>Email address</li>
  <li>Phone number</li>
  <li>City, canton/state, country</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>b) Billing data (if applicable):</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>Billing name, company, address</li>
  <li>VAT/Tax ID (for corporate invoicing)</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>c) Professional profile data (optional, provided voluntarily):</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>Job title, department, organisation name</li>
  <li>Short biography, LinkedIn profile URL</li>
  <li>Profile photo</li>
  <li>Skills/expertise, languages spoken</li>
  <li>Industry sector, company size</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>d) Preference and consent data:</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>Dietary requirements, accessibility needs</li>
  <li>Profile visibility settings (public / connections only / hidden)</li>
  <li>Communication preferences and marketing opt-in</li>
  <li>Data sharing preferences (sponsors, exhibitors, networking)</li>
  <li>Photography/media consent</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>e) Technical and transactional data:</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>Ticket type, order number, payment status</li>
  <li>QR code token (for event access)</li>
  <li>Check-in timestamp and method</li>
  <li>Consent timestamps</li>
</ul>

<h3>4. Purposes &amp; Legal Basis</h3>
<table>
  <tr><th>Purpose</th><th>Legal Basis</th></tr>
  <tr><td>Fulfilling the registration and providing event access</td><td>Performance of contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR / Art. 31 nDSG)</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Processing payments via Stripe</td><td>Performance of contract</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Issuing invoices and complying with tax obligations</td><td>Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR)</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Sending event-related communications (confirmation, reminders, logistics)</td><td>Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR / Art. 34 nDSG)</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Badge generation and QR code access control</td><td>Performance of contract</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Sending marketing communications about future events</td><td>Explicit consent (opt-in)</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Sharing contact data with sponsors or exhibitors</td><td>Explicit consent (opt-in)</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Publishing event photography and video</td><td>Explicit consent (registration checkbox)</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Internal analytics and attendance reporting</td><td>Legitimate interest</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Audit trail for compliance and dispute resolution</td><td>Legitimate interest / legal obligation</td></tr>
</table>

<h3>5. Third-Party Data Processors</h3>
<p>We share personal data only with the following categories of service providers, acting as data processors under written agreements:</p>
<table>
  <tr><th>Service Provider</th><th>Purpose</th><th>Data Shared</th><th>Location</th></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>Stripe, Inc.</strong></td><td>Payment processing (PCI DSS Level 1)</td><td>Order total, currency, customer email, billing address</td><td>USA / EU (adequacy decision)</td></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>Email delivery service</strong></td><td>Transactional emails (confirmations, tickets)</td><td>Recipient email, name, email content</td><td>Per provider (EU/US)</td></tr>
  <tr><td><strong>Infomaniak Network SA</strong></td><td>Database and application hosting</td><td>All registration data (stored in our database)</td><td>Switzerland</td></tr>
</table>
<p>Where data is transferred outside Switzerland or the EEA, we ensure adequate safeguards are in place (adequacy decisions, Standard Contractual Clauses, or equivalent mechanisms under Art. 16 nDSG).</p>

<p><strong>Optional data sharing with sponsors/exhibitors:</strong> If you explicitly opt in during registration (checkbox: "Allow exhibitor/sponsor contact" or "Share my info with sponsors"), your name, email, job title, and company may be shared with event sponsors or exhibitors for the purpose of event-related follow-up. You may withdraw this consent at any time.</p>

<h3>6. Data Retention</h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Registration data:</strong> retained for <strong>2 years</strong> after the event for reporting, follow-up, and re-invitation purposes, then permanently deleted.</li>
  <li><strong>Payment records:</strong> retained for <strong>10 years</strong> as required by Swiss commercial and tax law (Art. 958f CO).</li>
  <li><strong>Audit logs:</strong> retained indefinitely as an immutable compliance trail (anonymised where possible).</li>
  <li><strong>Consent records:</strong> retained for the duration of the registration data retention period.</li>
  <li><strong>Marketing preferences:</strong> retained until you withdraw consent or the data retention period expires.</li>
</ul>

<h3>7. Your Rights</h3>
<p>Under the nDSG and GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Right of access:</strong> request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.</li>
  <li><strong>Right to rectification:</strong> request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.</li>
  <li><strong>Right to erasure:</strong> request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention obligations). Upon an erasure request, we will nullify your personal data in our records and log the action in our audit trail.</li>
  <li><strong>Right to restrict processing:</strong> request that we limit how we use your data.</li>
  <li><strong>Right to data portability:</strong> receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.</li>
  <li><strong>Right to object:</strong> object to processing based on legitimate interest.</li>
  <li><strong>Right to withdraw consent:</strong> withdraw any previously given consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.</li>
</ul>
<p>To exercise any of these rights, contact <strong>privacy@swiss-robotics.org</strong>. We will respond within <strong>30 days</strong>.</p>
<p>You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the <strong>Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC)</strong> in Switzerland, or with a supervisory authority in your country of residence if you are in the EEA.</p>

<h3>8. Cookies &amp; Tracking</h3>
<p>The SRAtix registration platform uses only <strong>essential, first-party cookies</strong> required for authentication and session management. We do not use third-party tracking cookies, analytics trackers, or advertising pixels.</p>
<table>
  <tr><th>Cookie</th><th>Purpose</th><th>Duration</th></tr>
  <tr><td>Session token</td><td>Authentication (logged-in users)</td><td>15 minutes</td></tr>
  <tr><td>Refresh token</td><td>Token renewal</td><td>7 days</td></tr>
</table>

<h3>9. Data Security</h3>
<p>We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Encrypted data transmission (TLS/HTTPS)</li>
  <li>Password hashing (bcrypt)</li>
  <li>Role-based access control with least-privilege principles</li>
  <li>Hosting on Swiss infrastructure (Infomaniak, Switzerland)</li>
  <li>HMAC-signed webhooks for inter-system communication</li>
  <li>Regular access reviews and audit logging</li>
</ul>

<h3>10. Changes to This Policy</h3>
<p>We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the event registration page. The current version is always available via the link in the registration form.</p>

<h3>11. Contact</h3>
<p>Swiss Robotics Association<br>
Zurich, Switzerland<br>
Email: <strong>privacy@swiss-robotics.org</strong></p>
```
